# Anywork
Anywork is an app that connects users with local cleaning service providers (Cleaning women, laundry ladies) it helps users find reliable service providers in their area, Users can request laundry pickup and deliver directly from their homes. it simplifies access to laundry services for busy individuals while helping local laundry providers earn more and expand their customer base. The vision of this app is to become the most trusted cleaning services marketplace in Nigeria, with verified efficient and empowered laundry providers through technology.  


PROBLEM STATEMENT

Many people, especially working professionals and students, struggle to find reliable cleaning services that can not only provide pick-up service but also offer the option to do the cleaning as a type of home service, at the same time many skilled laundry women struggle to find customers because they rely on word of mouth and local connections.

WHAT ANYWORK DOES?

Any work offers a platform that creates a two-sided marketplace that connects customers who need cleaning services with Service providers who are available nearby. Through the app users can request laundry pickup and delivery, Can book cleaning services for their apartments, service provdiders can view available requests, accept jobs, and get paid after completion.  
This system ensures transparency, trust and convenience for both parties. 

WHY IT MATTERS? 

It creates convenience for service users and it also empowers and opens service providers up to a wide range of users  
For users it saves time, reduces stress, and ensures clean clothes,  
For service providers it provides consistent income opportunities and access to more customers.

WHO IS IT FOR? 
Anywork is for everyone, it is for busy professionals and enterprneurs that can't find the time to commit to small chores, it is for students that would rather spend their time on their studies, it is for families with large volumes of laundry or big living spaces, it is for the elderly or physically limited individuals. it is also for local service providers that are looking to start out, build or expand their client base.

KEY FEATURES 
1. user profiles: both users and service providers can register, set up their profiles, and manage their accounts.
2. Location based matching: users are automatically matched with nearby service providers using GPS.
3. Order request and management: customers can request laundry pickup, specify clothing type, book cleaning services, and set delivery preferences.
4. Real time order tracking: users can track pickup, washing, and delivery progress.
5. Chat and call functionality: in-app communication between users and providers.
6. Secure payment integration: users pay online (via Paystack or Flutterwave), and providers get credited after completion.
7. Ratings & Reviews: helps ensure consistent service quality.
8. Order History & Receipts:users can view past transactions and download receipts.
9. Notifications: reminders for pickup times and delivery updates.
