# Anywork
Anywork is an app that connects users with local cleaning service providers (Cleaning women, laundry ladies) it helps users find reliable service providers in their area, Users can request laundry pickup and deliver directly from their homes. it simplifies access to laundry services for busy individuals while helping local laundry providers earn more and expand their customer base. 


PROBLEM STATEMENT

Many people, especially working professionals and students, struggle to find reliable cleaning services that can not only provide pick-up service but also offer the option to do the cleaning as a type of home service, at the same time many skilled laundry women struggle to find customers because they rely on word of mouth and local connections.

What Anywork does?

Any work offers a platform that creates a two-sided marketplace that connects customers who need cleaning services with Service providers who are available nearby. Through the app users can request laundry pickup and delivery, Can book cleaning services for their apartments, service provdiders can view available requests, accept jobs, and get paid after completion, this system ensures transparency, trust and convenience for both parties.
